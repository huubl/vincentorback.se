Don’t work for free!

20 feb 2014

When starting out as a young designer/developer, it can be hard to evaluate your own work. So much that it sometimes feels right to work for free. It’s something I’ve felt a lot of times when I started and it can be because of the small size of the work or because the employer is a relative, a friend or most often a friend of a friends friend.

Even if you after the hundreds of hours you’ve spent learning your craft feel like the tasks is simple, remember that they don’t and that’s why they need your help. Most of the times it’s a business that needs help and if that business makes money, they have money.

Now of course there are times when working for free can really benefit you. Here’s a lovely chart made by the lovely Jessica Hische giving you guidance for when to work for free and when to just say no.
