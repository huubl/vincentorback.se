Arcini

arcini.com

Nikolina Arcini is an excellent photographer who takes amazing pictures and she specializes in horse photography

Personal portfolios is always my favorite website to make. It gives you a lot more freedom and possibilities to test new techniques and perspectives. The website should reflect the person that puts it’s name on it which at first can be a bit tricky, but it’s what gives the site feel unique in the end.

The result is focus on large photos without useless clutter.

The site if built with WordPress as CMS to make it easy for Nikolina to update new albums, information, news, sell prints etc. There are lots of custom settings the make it easy to customize pages when needed.

Portfolios has a tendency to get filled with large and heavy images. You want them to look crisp with high resolution but you’ve got to look out for getting stuck with too long time for pages to load. This is why I’ve made it easy for Nikolina to optimize her images when uploaded to the site.

The album images are also “lazy loaded” which means they aren’t loaded until the visitor scrolls the page. This lets the page load faster and the images load when they are needed.
