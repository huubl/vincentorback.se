Prophet Cruzin

www.prophetcruz.in

Prophet Cruzin’ is a Swedish lifestyle company designing products that makes everyday life neater.

My work with Prophet Cruzin started almost at the same time as my friend Adam had the idea for the company. He wanted to create his own community with things like music, clothes, movies and more.

The site is still in development and at the moment the store only holds one product, the Cruzer. A transparent cruise board.

The main site is built with WordPress as a CMS to easily update with events, photos and more. The store functionality is built with the e-commerce platform Tictail using a custom theme.
