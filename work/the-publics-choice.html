The Public’s Choice

publikensval.nordiskamuseet.se

The Public’s Choice is an initiative form the Nordic Museum in Stockholm that wanted to give visitors the chance to vote for what they should exhibit next.

The Nordic Museum chose 55 exhibition objects from their collection which were voted for by it’s visitors, both inside the museum and online.

The project consisted of three websites. Two were inside the museum on two large touch monitors and one was available online as a responsive site.

It was a great deal of fun working with large touch monitors because it enabled a whole new range of features which we could use. Such as not having to place every object within the viewport but instead place objects at random and let the user explore themselves.

The website works great on tablets and smartphones because they use the same touch features as the touch screens in the museum.
