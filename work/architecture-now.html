Architecture Now / Stockholm

arkitekturnustockholm.arkdes.se

The Swedish Architecture and design center wanted a grand guide to the best new architecture in Stockholm.

Architecture Now Stockholm is your guide to the latest architecture in Stockholm. By exploring some of the most acclaimed projects in recent years, you’ll get to know a city in the midst of a process of rapid transformation. The applications and website are designed and developed at my previous employer, the web agency Queensbridge.

Architecture Now is and iPhone and Android application as well as a website which makes it accessible everywhere, even offline.

Building the app was of course both learning and challenging. Since I’m not an app developers, but a web developers, PhoneGap was the perfect tool for what we wanted to do. PhoneGap is in short a great framework for packaging websites and convert that website into both an iPhone and Android application.

By having the website as a mobile application, we could offer users all texts and pictures, saving their favorites and use the map offline.
