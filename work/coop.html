Coop

www.coop.se

Coop is the next biggest grocery retail in Sweden and is cooperative owned by it’s over three million members.

It’s been a great honor working for Coop. It’s not just a good store, but also a company with great environmental products and policies. It’s also a store that you get a personal connection to.

This surely hasn’t been a one man project. The entire website concept, design and development is made by the digital agency Daytona and my work started after the launch in the fall of 2012. Coop was one of the first larger companies in the world to have a responsive website. My work with Coop started in the fall of 2013 and kept going until the spring of 2015.

Coop has over 3.3 million members and the website is used by approximately 2.6 million users each month.
