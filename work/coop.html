Coop

www.coop.se

Coop is the next biggest grocery retail in Sweden and is cooperative owned by it’s over three million members.

It’s been a great honor working for Coop. It’s not just a good store, but also a company with great environmental products and policies. It’s also a store that you get a personal connection to.

This surely hasn’t been a one man project. The entire concept, design and development was made by the digital agency Daytona. My work started after the launch in the fall of 2012. Coop was one of the first larger companies in the world to have a responsive website. I worked with Coop from 2013 to 2015.

Coop has over 3.4 million members and the website is used by approximately 2.6 million users each month.

Coop - Handla Hemma

“Handla Hemma” is Coops online shopping platform for ordering groceries to your doorstep. We made a total renew of the front-end experience making the site finally responsive and really fast.

The site is sort of a single-page application in that it’s really fast to navigate through the product listings, products, the cart etc. since they are all loaded asyncronosly.

It was fun creating a full website with lots of content and services that works great on smartphones and up to really huge screens. The content was easily organized and I think it worked out great!

It was a lot of work, but with a great team we pulled through! Check out the result at coop.se/handla-hemma
