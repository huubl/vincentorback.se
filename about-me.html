About me

Hi there!

I'm Vincent. A designer and developer living in Stockholm. I work at the digital agency Daytona where i do front-end development. I love building responsive, responsible websites and designing things smarter and easier. When I'm not doing client work i usually tinker with side projects.

Here are some things I love to read and listen to in order to learn more about the web.

Books

Design is a job

Mike Monterio goes deep into the web designer work experience, writing how fun or terrifying it can be when working with customers, both working at an agency or freelancer. He share tips like how important it is to not be an asshole and how you can make sure you get paid for what your doing. www.abookapart.com

The Manual

The Manual is a thrice-yearly publication with stories from six people working as designers, developers, etc. Stories that explore what we do and who we are as designers. The book does not contain any code, screenshots or tutorials. It's just stories from real life that connects to the work design business in the most fascinating ways.www.alwaysreadthemanual.com

Conversations with Design Entrepreneurs

This book has it all. Amazing interviews with some of the smartest, most interesting and driven people whom all got their own start-up. The book is filled with beutifull illustrations and it's just so good looking. Buy it just to touch it, it's worth it! www.kernandburnbook.com

Podcasts

ShopTalk

Chris Coyier and Dave Rupert has a fun and educating podcast with loads of jokes, sound effects and great guests. www.shoptalkshow.com

The WebAhead

Jen Simmons interviews guests every week about the future of the web. The subjects are very different each week and it feels like she always gets the most interesting people that are really passionate about their professions. www.5by5.tv/webahead

The Big Web Show

The king of internet Jeffrey Zeldman interview different people about the web industry about web publication, content strategy, typography, web techniques and much more. www.muleradio.net/thebigwebshow
