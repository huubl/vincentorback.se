 About me

I’m Vincent Orback, a freelancing web designer and front-end developer based in Stockholm, Sweden.

The work I do ranges from tiny portfolios to huge E-commerce platforms. I love building well engineered, high performing websites with appealing designs. My main focus is interface development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and design but also work with React, PHP, Node, WordPress and a whole lot of other web technologies.

If you want to talk, send me an email!

Experience: Currently freelancing, previously web developer at Earth People and before that interface developer at Daytona.

Worked with: Coop, Systembolaget, Arkitektur Förlag, Nordiska Museet, Arkitektur- och Designcentrum, International Campaign to Abolish Nuclear Weapons, Rikskriscentrum and lots more.

Specialties: Front-end, WordPress, prototyping, E-commerce.
