 About me

I’m Vincent Orback, a freelancing web developer and designer based in Stockholm, Sweden.

The work I do ranges from tiny portfolios to huge E-commerce platforms. I love building well engineered, high performing websites with appealing designs. My main focus is interface development and design but I’m also used to working with Node, React, WordPress, PHP and a whole lot of other web technologies.

Worked with: Arkitektur Förlag, Nordiska Museet, Coop, Systembolaget, International Campaign to Abolish Nuclear Weapons, Djurens Rätt, 1000 möjligheter, Arkitektur- och Designcentrum, Sveriges Författarförbund, Välj att sluta, Manscentrum, Rikskriscentrum and lots more.

Experience: Currently freelancing, previously web developer at Earth People and before that interface developer at Daytona.

Work well with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, WordPress, E-commerce, prototyping, having fun.
